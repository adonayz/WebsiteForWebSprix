Teleconferencing Services

Phone conferencing is a way for people to discuss business over the phone. Our conference bridge allows our clients to join a conference call in two ways. Either someone calls into a meeting using a dedicated phone number and a passcode (which helps the call manager control who hears and receives the information on the call) or our automated system calls the person and other members of a group simultaneously and put everyone on a conference bridge as they answer.

Teleconferencing Features

How it works

Conference Calling
  • With our system, you will not need a computer or internet connection to attend the calls and either a mobile or landline phone is the only appliance required.
    		•
  • You can initiate a conference call for your group with just a single SMS and whenever you want to.
    		•
  • Our automated system will notify you and others members of your group via SMS that you will soon join a conference call.
    		•
  • The system then calls you and other members of your group simultaneously and put everyone on a conference bridge as they answer.
    		•
  • You as the host can moderate the conference with the active conference management console.
    		•
  • You can see the list of conference attendees that are dialed into the bridge.
    		•
  • You can put the conference on lecture mode muting all except the speaker.
    		•
  • You can also eject or kick conference attendees whenever you want.
    		•
  • You can dial out to other people to join the conference in progress.
    		•
  • You can optionally record the conference free for archiving purposes.
    		•
