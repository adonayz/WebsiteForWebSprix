Our Story

WebSprix was established in June 2011 and became operational in March 2013 as a premier provider of Mobile Value Added Services (MVAS) in Ethiopia with offerings of next generation SMS and IVR business solutions helping our clients to connect better with their customers. We offer services ranging from basic bulk SMS to IVR Based complete business solutions. Our MVAS business solutions include Bulk SMS, Voice Broadcasting with multi language support, Call Conferencing, SMS Marketing, IVR and SMS-Based Polling, among others.

Assessing the market, our clients’ needs and our capabilities, the founders of the company decided to broaden the company’s services portfolio and WebSprix has evolved to become a Total Information Technology Solutions Provider. WebSprix specializes in helping organizations create dynamic and scalable infrastructures. We help companies move away from siloed servers, storage, information, and processes to embracing converged, virtualized, and cloud-based technologies. We design, install and support advanced solutions on a turnkey basis from supplying reliable hardware and appropriate software to providing support throughout the useful lifecycle of the solution. From existing network optimization and upgrade to complex design and implementation, we provide expert solutions and outstanding customer service for businesses of any size in Ethiopia.
