Personalized Bulk SMS Services

Bulk SMS sends a general message to 10s, 100s, 1000s and millions of your contacts at a click. As wonderful as it is for mass messaging, bulk SMS lacks personalization and ability to capture unique details with respect to each recipient.

Our Bulk SMS software tool allows our clients to send fully personalized SMS messages so that they can address their target audience directly in a unique and personal way. As a business, you would want to make your message as relevant as possible to your customers, to maximize your chances to attract their attention, and sending a personalized text message is a great way to start.

Personalized Bulk SMS Features

You can create your SMS by merging different columns in you Excel sheet & upload different contact lists for different campaigns. The system shows you a preview your message before sending.

Example: If an insurance company wants to remind its customers about premium due date, it can send personalized bulk SMS by mentioning the name of each recipient with his premium amount due and payment due date:

Dear Ato Alemu, your premium of ETB 500.00 is due on 15th September, 2014.
