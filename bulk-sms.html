Bulk SMS Services

Our premium-quality bulk SMS service lets our clients to send large quantities of SMS messages to individuals who have opted in to receive information from them with ease. WebSprix Bulk SMS Service allows national reach to prospects and customers alike at a fraction of the effort and cost compared to voice communications.

Bulk SMS Features

Details about our service
  • Our bulk SMS service allows you to substantially extend your sales, marketing, operations and internal communications.
    		•
  • You can add contacts via uploading a CSV file, or if you have few contacts, you can type them in manually.
    		•
  • Once you have added you contacts list and created groups, you can send your messages either from your PC or from your personal mobile phone.

    What Kinds of Businesses Use Bulk SMS?

    Banks and Other Businesses that Provide Financial Services

    A bank may issue an advisory to all their clients that server maintenance is being done, and online banking services will be temporarily affected.

    Retail Companies

    Supermarkets, bookstores, and other retail companies use bulk SMS to send alerts on special events such a sale, promotions, and other marketing messages.

    Cargo Distribution and Delivery Companies

    Bulk SMS are very useful to cargo distribution and delivery companies, particularly in updating delivery schedules and tracking package movement.

    Travel and Hospitality Sectors

    Airlines, passenger and cargo shipping companies, hotels, and restaurants can use bulk SMS for reservations, promotions, and confirmations.
